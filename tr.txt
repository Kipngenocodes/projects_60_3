Hopsin, born Marcus Jamal Hopson, is an American rapper, producer, and director known for his intense lyrical style and thought-provoking themes. Emerging in the 2000s with his sharp lyricism and socially conscious content, Hopsin gained popularity with songs addressing personal struggles, societal issues, mental health, and industry critiques. His music often reflects themes of personal conflict, self-awareness, and frustration with mainstream hip-hop culture, making him a unique voice in the independent rap scene.
